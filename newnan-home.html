The Project

100+ stories. One tornado. A single night made from both.

In March 2021, an EF-4 tornado moved through Newnan, Georgia, and tore open the lives of thousands of people. In the years that followed, creator and director Gwendolyn Kuhlmann collected more than one hundred recorded voices from the community — survivors, first responders, case workers, children, elders, and faith leaders.

Those voices became Seasons of Strength: a ninety-minute live performance weaving oral history, a symphony orchestra, contemporary dance, and projected documentary video into a single experience.

The score is built on Max Richter's Recomposed: The Four Seasons across Acts II–V. Act I opens on improvisations of Alan Jackson's “Chattahoochee” and Isabella Ivy's Sirens: Voices from the Heavens.
